Introduction

The goal of the Utah Constitution Party is to spread the message of “Honoring God, Defending the Family, and Restoring the Republic to the principles given us by the Founding Fathers”. This is the same message fostered by the Founding Fathers. They understood that to remain a free and prosperous nation their message needed to be embedded in the hearts and minds of each succeeding generation of Americans.

If you're interested in participating in our party proceedings and having a voice in things such as future party leadership and nomination of candidates, please come and join us for our county caucus and convention.

This meeting is for all members of the Utah Constitution Party in Cache County no matter what precinct you are in. This meeting will serve as both our caucus and our convention.

Those who are not a member of the party are also welcome to come and observe. All are welcome.

State Convention

During our meeting, will be electing delegates for the Utah Constitution Party state convention.

The state convention will be held on April 25th, 2020 in Salt Lake City.

The state convention proceedings will include items such as:

  • Endorsing candidates for statewide office.
  • Electing National Convention delegates.
  • Approving or amending the State Party Constitution or Bylaws.
  • Adopting or revising the State Party Platform.
  • Addressing other appropriate issues.

The only requirements to serve as a state delegate is to be a current member of the party and a willingness to serve.

County Executive Committee

During our meeting, will be electing a new county executive committee.

This committee includes the following positions:

  • County Chairman
  • County Vice Chairman
  • County Secretary
  • County Treasurer
  • 3 District Chairmen (North, Central, South)

Ideally, we would like to fill all of these positions. At the very least, we need a County Chairman in order to remain an active party affiliate.

In the past, our executive committe has done things such as running booths at fairs and shows, supporting other freedom-minded groups in the area, and supporting candidates for office. It can be a great way to get involved and help spread the message of liberty and the Constitution.

The time commitment for each of these positions is light. It probably averages 1-2 hours a month.

If you are interested in being a member of our executive committee, please come and join us. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions about these positions.
