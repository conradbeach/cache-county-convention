Video Conference Details

How to Join

For our meeting, we will be using Cisco Webex. You don't need a Cisco Webex account to join the meeting. To join the meeting from your computer, visit this link.

You can also connect using their iPhone/iPad app or with the Android app.

When you go to the link, you'll be prompted to download and install the Cisco Webex desktop app. You can do this if you want, or you can click "Cancel" and join from your web browser. If you choose to join from your web browser, click "Cancel" and then click the "Join from your browser" link. It may take a couple of seconds to show up on the page.

You will be asked to enter your name and email to join the call. Please enter accurate information here so we have an accurate list of attendees.

If you are prompted for a password, enter "constitution".

Once you join the call, keep please your microphone muted so the call doesn't get noisy. If you'd like a chance to speak, please click the "Raise Hand" button or comment in the chat.

You're welcome to comment in the chat at any time and communicate with the other participants of the meeting.

Need Help?

We'd encourage you to connect to the video conference at least 10 or 15 minutes before the meeting so you have time to install software and resolve any technical issues you may encounter. You may even want to get set up well in advance of the meeting if you're unfamiliar with video conferencing software. Feel free to contact our county chair, Conrad Beach, with any questions or problems. (His contact details are on the bottom of this page.)
